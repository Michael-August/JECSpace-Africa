About Us

JECspace Africa is a digital/tech company that aims at providing digital/tech solutions to individuals. Our aim is to connect people and technology. We use innovative and user-friendly ideas to create products and services that are useful, enjoyable, easy to access and affordable. We are also committed in promoting the integration of science, technology, and math in our daily lives. At JECspace, we help people to access the best technology at a click of button, we help people learn, keep up-to-date with the latest technology ideas and knowledge, as well as manage and deploy digital strategies for businesses.

Our Vision

We aspire to be the number one African innovation and technology brand, emerging from the South East.

Our Mission

To connect people and technology, help people to access the best technology at a click of button and also delivering business solutions for the web and mobile world with digital strategies.
